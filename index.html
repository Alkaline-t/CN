What is The Launchpad?

The Launchpad is an online career mentor platform where students can get a path to their career.

Why is it needed?

Assume a student wants to become a Photographer, and in their surroundings, nobody knows how to become a Photographer and what the right path is. A student researches on the internet. We all know the internet has a huge amount of data by different persons on their experiences and research data. The student is stuck with huge amounts of data. At this time, The Launchpad helps students by providing a roadmap to achieve their career.

Key features of The Launchpad:

  • Get the right and best roadmap for your career.
  • Virtual Career Experience (VCE)
  • Talk to Experts of their Career.

What is Virtual Career Experience (VCE)?

It provides an experience and type of work to students before pursuing their career.

What is Talk to Experts of their Career?

This feature clears the doubts of students, where students can talk to experienced experts of their career.
